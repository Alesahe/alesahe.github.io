💙 a little museum 💙

the wall behind you gives out, making way for a rough cobblestone path. neatly trimmed bushes line the two sides near the start, where you are.

and on either side, tall, haphazard cardboard buildings stand. you walk to the first – it's one from the inside of a pram, being pushed along to the park.

the second has mint green walls and a black-and-white striped mattress. and a cot. inside, a stack of books and a sleeping child.

the third: a scene, a young child and a teacher and a sea of hundreds of people. a brown paper bag is waved around.

you look around.
